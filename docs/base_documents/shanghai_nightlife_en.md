# Shanghai Nightlife

When the sun sets, Shanghai transforms into a dazzling playground. The Bund lights up with colonial-era buildings glowing in gold and white, while across the river the Lujiazui skyline pulses with LED spectacles. Bar Rouge on the Bund rooftop offers panoramic views and champagne-fueled parties until dawn. In the Former French Concession, speakeasies hide behind unmarked doors, with Flask serving craft cocktails through a vintage Coca-Cola machine. Found 158 underground plaza in Jingan hosts live jazz and electronic music events most weekends. The Xintiandi district blends restored shikumen architecture with upscale lounges and late-night dining. For a more local experience, the night markets near Shouning Road come alive with sizzling seafood stalls and cold Tsingtao beer. Karaoke culture runs deep, and massive KTV palaces like Cashbox Party World stay open until six in the morning, private rooms echoing with Mandarin pop hits.
